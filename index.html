Background

The writer
behind the work.

Aaron Raja — Bengaluru

I'm Aaron — a content writer with a slightly unusual background. I studied aerospace engineering, published research with IEEE and Springer, and then spent the last several years writing for some of the most diverse industries imaginable.

What that's taught me is that good content isn't about knowing the jargon. It's about understanding the reader and making complexity feel approachable.

At Fragomen Educational Services, I built the content strategy from the ground up — long-form blog articles, social media copy, webinar materials, and decks that turned a niche service into something students actually engaged with.

Before that, as a freelancer, I was producing 100+ pieces of content per month for clients across B2B SaaS, travel, EdTech, and tech — simultaneously managing social calendars for 13+ brands while hitting SEO targets and deadlines.

I'm particularly drawn to SaaS writing. It sits at the intersection of my strengths: technical depth, clear explanation, and storytelling that turns product features into genuine value propositions.
